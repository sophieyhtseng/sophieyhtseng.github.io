What is SDG 14?

SDG 14 focuses on conserving and sustainably using oceans, seas, and marine resources for sustainable development. Oceans are vital to our planet's health and economy.

Why is it Important?

Oceans cover 70% of Earth's surface and provide over half of the oxygen we breathe. They are home to diverse marine life but are under threat from pollution, overfishing, and climate change.
